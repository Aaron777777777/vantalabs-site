ABOUT

Built around clarity, restraint and useful products.

Vanta Labs is an independent UK software company focused on building clean digital products with a simple idea at the core: remove the noise, keep what matters, and make the experience feel sharp, useful and intentional.

We believe good software should feel clear from the first second. That means stronger fundamentals, less clutter, better visual restraint, and products that solve real problems without unnecessary complexity.

Our work is guided by premium design, practical execution, and a bias toward simple systems that are easy to understand and easy to use.
